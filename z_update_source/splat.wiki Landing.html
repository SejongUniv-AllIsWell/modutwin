3D Gaussian Splatting

splat.wiki is a crowdsourced platform for building digital twins of the real world with 3D Gaussian Splatting.  Upload a video, place it on the map, and it becomes part of a public atlas.

01 · How to participate

Ways to add your record

i.

Upload a video

Drop in a phone or drone clip — we run frame extraction, SfM, and training. Your scene joins the atlas as soon as it converges.

Upload video →
ii.

Submit a point cloud

Already trained your splat? Upload the artefact directly — .ply, .sog, or .splat. Geo-tag it and it's instantly viewable.

Upload point cloud →
iii.

Contribute images + SfM

Bring your own captures and COLMAP / OpenSfM output. We pick up the reconstruction from there and credit you on the resulting scene.

Upload images & SfM →
